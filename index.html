About Me

First SWE Internship

As a high school senior, I worked on implementing a driving simulator with a digital twin of Roosevelt Island. Then, I pursued my own research project, to determine the optimal model architecture for the classification of urban audio.

Personal Project:
Review Central Chrome Extension

Recently, I've been developing a chrome extension that aims to provide a better review system for e-commerce websites. Users are able to ask questions on the product page, and the most relevant review to answer that question will be fetched. Also, users can ask for AI-powered summaries or help in writing a review, to provide the best experience.

Hackathon Spotlight:
Edu-Ar-do

At MHacks x Google 2024, I worked with a team of passionate and talented peers to create an app, that allows for animations to be projected into camera view, with simple voice commands. We envisioned it as an education tool, allowing people to learn by seeing.
