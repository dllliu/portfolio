Roosevelt Island:
First SWE Internship

As a high school senior, I worked on implementing a driving simulator with a digital twin of Roosevelt Island. Then, I pursued my own research project, examining how the choice of Convolutional Neural Network (CNN) architecture affects classification of urban audio.

Hackathon Project Spotlight:
EduARdo IOS App

At MHacks x Google 2024, I worked with a team of passionate and talented peers to create an app, that allows for animations to be projected into camera view, with simple voice commands. We envisioned it as an education tool, allowing people to learn by seeing.

Hobby Highlight:

Most recently, I've been in Colorado for an internship at a national lab. Hiking has been a really something I have found I really enjoy. Some notable highlights are: I finished the Manitou Incline (over 2000 feet of elevation gain in 0.88 miles) round-trip in around 80 minutes round-trip, climbed to over 12000 feet in Rocky Mountain National Park, and visited the summit of Pikes Peak (over 14000 feet).
